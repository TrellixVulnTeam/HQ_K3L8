What is HQ?

HQ stands for headquarters - Literally a place for your mind. My philsophy is that good organization and planning are the keys to accomplishing all of your goals. HQ is a tool I created to help myself track all my projects and crush goals. Sick of looking at excel sheets and having lists scattered in different areas, I needed something to manage these lists for me without wasting my own time just getting organized. With varying interests, I needed a tool to help me focus on the right things at the right time in order to acheive all my goals.

What does HQ run on?

HQ is written in python on a web framework called Django. The backend is a MySQL database. I am a self-learner without any programming background and built this from watching youtube videos and reading online documentation. This would not be possible without the help of many users on stack overflow and the excellent documentation from the Django team.
